## 15. FAQs

**Essential FAQs**:

**Q: How is this different from just using ChatGPT?**
A: ChatGPT requires you to copy/paste content and loses context after a few messages. We automatically extract YouTube transcripts, scrape websites, parse PDFs, and keep everything organized on a visual canvas. The AI always has full context.

**Q: Do you train AI models on my data?**
A: No. Your canvases and conversations are private. We don't use your data for model training.

**Q: What happens when I run out of credits?**
A: You'll see a notification before you run out. Top up anytime with transparent pricing. Free tier includes enough credits for ~500 AI messages per month.

**Q: Can I use my own API keys for AI models?**
A: Not yet, but it's on our roadmap. Currently we handle all AI model access through our credit system.

**Q: How does team collaboration work?**
A: Create an organization, invite team members. Everyone with access to a canvas sees real-time updates. Anyone can continue any chat thread. Credits are shared at the org level.

**Q: What file types can I upload?**
A: PDFs work now. We're adding Google Docs, Word docs, images, and spreadsheets soon.

**Q: Is there a limit to canvas size?**
A: No. Canvases are infinite. Add as many nodes as you want.

**Q: Can I export my canvases?**
A: Yes. Export as JSON, PDF, or image. Chat transcripts export as markdown.

**Advanced/Positioning FAQs**:

**Q: Why not just use Miro/Figma + ChatGPT separately?**
A: You could, but you'd spend hours copying content between tools. We built the integration so AI natively understands your canvas context. Plus, Miro/Figma don't extract YouTube transcripts or scrape websites.

**Q: Is this for technical users only?**
A: No. If you can drag and drop files, you can use this. The infinite canvas might feel new for 5 minutes, then it's intuitive.

**Q: What's your uptime/reliability?**
A: Built on Convex (99.9% uptime). Real-time sync in <50ms. We've processed 50,000+ YouTube videos without issues.

**Q: Do you support SSO/SAML?**
A: Coming Q2 2025 for Organization plan.

**Q: What's your security/compliance situation?**
A: SOC 2 Type II in progress. GDPR compliant. Data encrypted at rest and in transit. Happy to fill out security questionnaires.
